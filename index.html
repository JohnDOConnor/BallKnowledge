How to Play

Guess the hidden World Cup player from the team sheet provided on the right!

Color Codes & Clues:

  • Green Direct exact match.
  • Yellow Close approximation or shared attribute:
    • Club/League: Plays in the same league country.
    • Age: Within ±2 years.
    • Height: Within ±5 cm.
    • Caps: Within ±10 appearances.
  • Red No match / completely outside variance range.

Directional Arrows:

The symbols ↑ and ↓ indicate if the target player's true values are higher or lower than your submitted guess.
